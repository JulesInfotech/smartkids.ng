Who We Are?

Our uniqueness is in our potent combination of body fitness, technology and financial literacy.

Technology can be empowering for kids of all ages, with tools that help children learn in fun and engaging ways, express their creativity and stay connected.

Smart Kids Zone is an Action Based Learning Centre conceptualized to prepare young people (4-17 years) for the challenges of the 21st century and getting them innovation ready by providing fun-filled energetic environment for kids where technology, physical fitness and entrepreneurship education intersect.

Our Philosophy

Smart Kids Zone believes the quote that says “All work and no play makes jack a dull boy”. Children need to experience the fun part of technology while also learning. Here, they learn creative skills like financial literacy to equip them in finance management, they also play games to boost their learning capabilities and perform other fun activities.

Why Coding for Kids?

Coding Aids in the Teaching of Digital Literacy
Understanding and engaging with technology is no longer a luxury in the modern world; it is a requirement for navigating an increasingly digitized society. Children must understand technology; in other words, they must be digitally literate.

Learning to code is a skill that is highly valued in the workforce & jobs related to computer programming is growing exponentially. Beyond the computer, coding teaches critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity and resilience.
